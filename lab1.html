Welcome to twin cities ice cream!

...think of us as your guide to the best ice cream in the twin cities

Izzy's Ice Cream is my favorite ice cream parlor in the twin cities. I have many memories of summertime walks to the location on Marshall Ave. in Saint Paul. The location is ideal for getting your ice cream and taking a nice walk around the area. I strongly recommend trying out anything with coffee in it, as Izzy's pairing with Peace coffee is fantastic!
